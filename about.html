About Remti

The AP tool we wish we
had, back when we ran
a 40-person bakery.

Remti started in 2023 when our founders — then running a growing hospitality group — spent every Tuesday in six different bank portals. So we built our own.

We think paying suppliers should take 15 minutes, not an afternoon. And the team doing it shouldn't have to touch a spreadsheet.

Most AP software either holds your money (giving you one more account to reconcile) or pretends to be a neo-bank (and expects you to switch). Remti does neither.

We're an orchestration layer on top of Open Banking. Your money stays in your Barclays, HSBC, or NatWest. We just make paying fifty suppliers feel like paying one.

We're small, we're profitable, and we're based in a converted warehouse in Bermondsey. Come say hello.

What we believe

Three things we won't budge on.

01

Your money, your bank.

We'll never ask you to fund an account with us. Your cash lives where you put it; we just help it leave correctly.

02

Boring, predictable pricing.

Per seat, per month. No transaction fees, no float, no hidden FX spread. What you see is the bill.

03

Made for finance teams.

Not finance-curious PMs. Every feature is shaped by AP managers, controllers, and FDs in weekly sessions.
