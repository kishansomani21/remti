Questions

Answered before you ask.

Does Remti hold my money?

No. Payments leave your own bank account directly via Open Banking. We never take custody of your funds — we just orchestrate the instructions.

Are there per-transaction fees?

None. You pay a flat fee per seat. Faster Payments are included at no cost. International payments have a small FX margin, disclosed before you send.

Which UK banks work with Remti?

All major UK business banks: Barclays, HSBC, Lloyds, NatWest, Santander, Monzo, Starling, Revolut, Tide, and more. If your bank supports Open Banking, we support you.

Can I try it before I pay?

Yes — 14 days free on any plan, including Business. No card required. Import suppliers, pay them, then decide.

How does billing work?

Monthly or annual, per seat. Annual saves 20%. Cancel any time; unused days are prorated on your next invoice.

Do you support multi-entity groups?

Yes, on the Scale plan. Each entity keeps its own suppliers, bank connections, and audit log — with a consolidated view across the group.
